[本地化] About iOS 14 Updates

2021-02-02

iOS 14.4

iOS 14.4 includes the following improvements for your iPhone:

  • Smaller QR codes can be recognized by Camera
  • Option to classify Bluetooth device type in Settings for correct identification of headphones for audio notifications
  • Notifications for when the camera on your iPhone is unable to be verified as a new, genuine Apple camera in iPhone 12, iPhone 12 mini, iPhone 12 Pro and iPhone 12 Pro Max

This release also fixes the following issues:

  • Image artifacts could appear in HDR photos taken with iPhone 12 Pro
  • Fitness widget may not display updated Activity data
  • Typing may be delayed and word suggestions may not appear in the keyboard
  • The keyboard may not come up in the correct language in Messages
  • Audio stories from the News app in CarPlay may not resume after being paused for spoken directions or Siri
  • Enabling Switch Control in Accessibility may prevent phone calls from being answered from the Lock Screen

For information on the security content of Apple software updates, please visit this website: https://support.apple.com/kb/HT201222

iOS 14.4 包括针对 iPhone 的以下改进：

  • “相机”可识别更小的二维码
  • “设置”中新增了分类蓝牙设备类型的选项，可正确识别耳机以便于音频通知
  • 在 iPhone 12、iPhone 12 mini、iPhone 12 Pro 和 iPhone 12 Pro Max 中，新增了 iPhone 上的相机无法验证为全新正品 Apple 相机时的通知功能

本更新还修复了以下问题：

  • iPhone 12 Pro 拍摄的 HDR 照片中可能出现伪像
  • “健身”小组件可能不显示更新的“健身记录”数据
  • 键盘键入可能出现延迟并且可能不显示字词建议
  • “信息”可能显示错误的键盘语言
  • “辅助功能”中启用了“切换控制”时可能无法从锁定屏幕接听电话

如需了解 Apple 软件更新的安全性内容，请访问此网站：https://support.apple.com/zh-cn/HT201222
